# Branches

## Parent and child branches

Combined parent and child branches are accessed as: {obj}`Note.branches`

## Parent branches

Parent branches are accessed as: {obj}`Note.branches.parents <Note.branches>`

## Child branches

Child branches are accessed as: {obj}`Note.branches.children <Note.branches>`

## Parent notes

Parent notes are accessed as: {obj}`Note.parents`

## Child notes

Child notes are accessed as: {obj}`Note.children`
